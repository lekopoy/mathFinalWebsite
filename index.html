Computer Bases

Computer Bases are a fundemental part of our society, with their being— binary, decimal, and hexadecimal. These are the primary systems used in computers, with each serving an important purpose.

Binary

Binary is a very prominent numerical system used in almost every computer. Binary is in the base-2 numeral system(bi- meaning two, twice or double. -nary refering to numerical states) In computer science it is applied by using the electrical states On & Off(1 & 0). One of these states is a BIT, When you take 8 bits, you get a BYTE.

We use transistors to make these electric states, they can either have electrical flow or not. These transistors allow us to actually make bits; we then build logic gates out of these transistors. Logic gates allow us to determine if certain conditions(different transistors status: on/off) are true or false.

Inside a modern day computer their are millions of transistors sitting inside your cpu.
